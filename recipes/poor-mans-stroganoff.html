Poor Man's Stroganoff

Description

Stroganoff a traditionally Russian dish made with egg noodles, beef, sour cream, and a sauce made from a mustard roux.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz package of egg noodles
  • 16 oz fresh sliced mushrooms
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 tsp Greek seasoning
  • 1 tsp vegetable oil
  • 1 pt sour cream
  • Salt & black pepper to taste
